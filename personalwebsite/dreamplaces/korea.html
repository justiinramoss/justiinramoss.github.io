South Korea

South Korea has always been on my bucket list because of its vibrant culture, delicious food, and scenery.

Seoul – The Heart of K-Culture

Seoul is a modern yet historical city, filled with skyscrapers, ancient palaces, and lively shopping streets.

  • Gyeongbokgung Palace – Walk through Korea’s grandest royal palace.
  • Myeongdong Shopping Street – A haven for Korean skincare, fashion, and street food!
  • Hongdae & Gangnam – Experience the nightlife and K-pop vibes.

Jeju Island – Nature’s Paradise

Jeju is South Korea’s tropical island, famous for its stunning landscapes, waterfalls, and beaches.

  • Hallasan Mountain – Hike South Korea’s highest peak.
  • Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak) – Enjoy a beautiful sunrise.
  • Jeongbang Waterfall – A stunning waterfall that drops directly into the ocean.

Busan – Beaches & Seafood Heaven

Busan is known for its beautiful beaches, fresh seafood, and stunning coastal views.

  • Haeundae Beach – Relax on Korea’s most famous beach.
  • Gamcheon Culture Village – Explore colorful, artistic streets.
  • Jagalchi Fish Market – Try Korea’s best fresh seafood!
