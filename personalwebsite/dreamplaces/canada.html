Canada

Canada is known for its stunning landscapes, diverse wildlife, and vibrant cities. Here are the top places I dream of visiting!

Banff National Park

Located in the Canadian Rockies, Banff is a paradise for nature lovers.

  • Lake Louise – A breathtaking turquoise lake surrounded by mountains.
  • Moraine Lake – One of the most photographed lakes in the world.
  • Banff Gondola – Get a stunning aerial view of the mountains.

Niagara Falls

One of the most famous waterfalls in the world, located on the border of Canada and the USA.

  • Horseshoe Falls – The largest and most powerful section of the falls.
  • Maid of the Mist – Get up close to the falls on a boat tour.
  • Niagara Falls at Night – Watch the colorful light show.

Toronto

Canada’s largest city, known for its iconic skyline and cultural diversity.

  • CN Tower – One of the tallest towers in the world with a glass floor observation deck.
  • Scotiabank Arena – Home of the Toronto Raptors and Maple Leafs.
  • Distillery District – A historic neighborhood filled with cafes, shops, and art galleries.
