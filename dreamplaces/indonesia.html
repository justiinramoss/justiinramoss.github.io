Indonesia

Indonesia is a tropical paradise with stunning beaches, ancient temples, and vibrant culture. Here are the top places I dream of visiting!

Bali

Bali is famous for its beautiful beaches, lush rice terraces, and spiritual atmosphere.

  • Kuta Beach – A perfect spot for surfing and relaxing on the sand.
  • Tegallalang Rice Terraces – Stunning green landscapes for the best nature photos.
  • Uluwatu Temple – A clifftop temple with breathtaking sunset views.

Borobudur Temple

The largest Buddhist temple in the world, an architectural wonder.

  • Ancient Stupas – Over 2,500 relief panels telling Buddhist stories.
  • Sunrise at Borobudur – A breathtaking experience seeing the temple glow at dawn.
  • Walk the Path of Enlightenment – A spiritual journey through history.

Komodo Island

Home of the legendary Komodo dragons and pristine pink sand beaches.

  • Komodo National Park – See the world’s largest lizards up close.
  • Pink Beach – A rare and beautiful beach with pink-colored sand.
  • Scuba Diving – Explore vibrant coral reefs and marine life.
