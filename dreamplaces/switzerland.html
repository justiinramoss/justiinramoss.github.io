Switzerland

Switzerland is a paradise for nature lovers, famous for its huge mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and charming villages.

Zermatt – Home of the Matterhorn

Zermatt is a stunning mountain town at the foot of the iconic Matterhorn.

  • Matterhorn – One of the most famous mountains in the world.
  • Gornergrat Railway – A scenic train ride with panoramic views.
  • Skiing & Snowboarding – Enjoy some of the best slopes in Europe.

Interlaken – Adventure Capital

Interlaken is a dream destination for adventure seekers, offering extreme sports and stunning nature.

  • Paragliding – Soar above the Swiss Alps for an unforgettable experience.
  • Lauterbrunnen Valley – A beautiful valley with waterfalls and hiking trails.
  • Lake Thun & Lake Brienz – Take a relaxing boat ride on turquoise waters.

Lucerne – A Fairytale City

Lucerne is a charming city known for its medieval architecture and breathtaking lake views.

  • Chapel Bridge – Walk across one of the oldest wooden bridges in Europe.
  • Mount Pilatus – Ride the world’s steepest cogwheel railway to the top.
  • Lake Lucerne – Cruise through the scenic Swiss landscape.
